And so, as we must if we are not to disturb the glow, we finish the walls with sand in a single neutral color... And from these delicate differences in the hue of the walls, the shadows in each room take on a tinge peculiarly their own. This is why we attach such importance to age and patina. A new painting, even one done in ink monochrome or subtle pastels, can quite destroy the shadows of an alcove, unless it is selected with the greatest care.
